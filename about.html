About

North Coast Permitting and Land Services, LLC provides practical land use and permitting support for projects throughout Lake County and Northern California.

We specialize in CEQA coordination, major use permits, building permits, and project feasibility. Our approach is straightforward: understand the project, identify the requirements, and guide it through the process efficiently.

With experience working with county planning departments and rural land use projects, we help landowners and applicants navigate complex regulations and avoid costly delays.
