About North Coast Permitting and Land Services

North Coast Permitting and Land Services provides land use consulting, environmental coordination, and permitting support for agricultural, rural, and development projects throughout Northern California.

Founded by Rick Steinberg and Mike, NCPS is built on years of hands-on experience in land management, agricultural operations, project coordination, permitting, and local agency communication.
