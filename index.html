Practical Permitting Help for Rural Landowners and Projects

North Coast Permitting and Land Services, LLC helps property owners, ranches, agricultural operations, and development projects navigate the permitting process from start to finish.

We specialize in CEQA coordination, Major Use Permits, agency communication, land use planning support, compliance strategy, and project management throughout Lake County and Northern California.

Services

CEQA Support

Assistance with environmental review coordination, project descriptions, agency communication, and required documentation.

Major Use Permits

Support preparing, organizing, and managing Major Use Permit applications for rural, agricultural, and commercial projects.

Agency Coordination

Communication with county planning departments, water boards, environmental agencies, and other regulatory offices.

Land Use Consulting

Site planning support, compliance strategy, property review, and practical guidance for moving projects forward.

Why Work With Us?

Permitting can be slow, confusing, and expensive when the process is not managed correctly. Our goal is to help clients understand what is required, organize the right information, communicate clearly with agencies, and keep projects moving.

We bring hands-on experience with rural properties, agricultural operations, land use permitting, compliance issues, and multi-agency coordination.
