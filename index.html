Practical Permitting Help for Rural Landowners and Projects

North Coast Permitting and Land Services, LLC helps property owners, ranches, agricultural operations, and development projects navigate the permitting process from start to finish.

We specialize in CEQA coordination, Major Use Permits, building permits, agency communication, land use planning support, and project feasibility throughout Lake County and Northern California.

Experience working with county planning departments, state agencies, and rural land use projects across Northern California.

Services

CEQA Support

Assistance with environmental review coordination, project descriptions, agency communication, and required documentation.

Major Use Permits

Support preparing, organizing, and managing Major Use Permit applications for rural, agricultural, and commercial projects.

Building Permits & Project Feasibility

We work with property owners to evaluate what they want to build, determine what can realistically be permitted, and map out the steps needed to move the project forward.

Agency Coordination

Communication with county planning departments, water boards, environmental agencies, and regulatory offices.

Land Use Consulting

Site planning support, compliance strategy, property review, and practical guidance for moving projects forward.

Why Work With Us?

Permitting can be slow, confusing, and expensive when it is not managed correctly. We help clients understand what is required, identify the right path forward, organize the necessary information, and communicate clearly with agencies.

Our approach is practical and direct. We help evaluate what can realistically be permitted, what approvals may be required, and what steps are needed to move a project forward.

Whether you are planning a rural property improvement, agricultural project, commercial use, or development proposal, we provide hands-on support from the early planning stage through agency coordination.
